<h2>Description</h2>

<p>AU Microscopii is a cool star located approximately <b>31</b> light years away. AU Microscopii is one of the youngest stars discovered, as its age is ~20 million years old. It is suspected that AU Microscopii may have a fourth planet, however more observations must be completed to prove the existence of <b>AU Microscopii e</b>. AU Microscopii is located near a binary system, AT Microscopii, implying it may be a part of it, forming a triple-star system system, like the system the North Star, Polaris is in.</p>
