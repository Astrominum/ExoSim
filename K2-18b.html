Description

K2-18b is a large Neptunian-type exoplanet with an atmosphere rich in hydrogen and likely an ocean, as well. It's quite massive, over 8 times that of Earth. In 2019, spectroscopy revealed water vapor in its atmosphere. In April 2025, JWST (James Webb Space Telescope) discovered traces of DMS, or dimethyl sulfide, in K2-18b's atmosphere. The only known source of DMS is marine organisms, like phytoplankton, which caused K2-18b to gain wide-spread attention as a possible source of alien-life.
