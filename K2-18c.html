Kepler-62f




Stats

  • Star: Kepler-62

  • Planet Type: Super Earth

  • Radius: 1.4 R⨁

  • Mass: 2.8 M⨁

  • Surface Temperature: 208K

  • Orbital Period: 267.3 days

  • Distance from star: 0.72 AU

  • Distance from Earth: 124 light-years

  • Discovery Date: 4/18/2013

  • Discovery Method: Transit Photometry

Description

Kepler-62f is an Earth-sized exoplanet located in its star's habitable zone. Research suggests that Kepler-62f has a unstable, CO²-dense atmosphere. Kepler-62f is likely rocky, with a composition of silicates and iron. Liquid water may be on the surface.
