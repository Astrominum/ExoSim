WD-1145+017




Stats

  • Planet(s): WD-1145+017b

  • Star Type: D Type White Dwarf

  • Diameter: 16,704km

  • Mass: 7.3 × 10²⁶ metric tons

  • Surface Temperature: 14,973K

  • Absolute Magnitude: 16.8

  • Distance from Earth: 567 light-years

  • Discovery Date: 10/21/2015

  • Discovery Method: Transit Photometry

Description

WD-1145+017 is a white dwarf type star. It is immensely dense, over 300,000 times the density of our Sun. WD-1145+017 is the first discovered white dwarf to have an exo-asteroid orbiting it. One scientist has referred to WD-1145+017 as a "zombie star" because it consumes the debris of its exo-asteroid. The stellar atmosphere around WD-1145+017 is rich in metal due to the disinigration of the objects orbiting it.
