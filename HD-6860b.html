Description

HD-6860b is a large, hot Gas Giant. It is incredibly heavy, as it is 20+ times more massive than Jupiter, the heaviest planet in our Solar System. It's also extremely hot, around the temperature of a Red Giant. By Gas Giant standards, HD-6860b is relatively small, only a little bit bigger than Jupiter. Many believe HD-6860b is a Brown Dwarf, a "failed star", due to it's size, temperature and mass.
