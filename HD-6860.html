Description

HD-6860 is a cool star located approximately 199 light years away. It is a relatively dim star, especially compared to the Sun. It is, however large, with its diameter being 86 times of that of the Sun. It would take exactly 199 years for you to reach HD-6860, if you were traveling at the speed of light. HD-6860 is a low to intermediate-mass Red Giant, meaning that it is in the last stages of its life and it is running out of hydrogen in its core to fuse. HD-6860 is still considered a recent discovery, as it was found in 2023.
