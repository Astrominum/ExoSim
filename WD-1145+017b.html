Description

WD-1145+017b is a minor planet, or exo-asteroid as some astronomers call it. Some believe that WD-1145+017b isn't the only major objects orbiting WD-1145+017, with other much smaller celestial bodies. The most popular theory is that all of these minor objects all stemmed from one major exoplanet, however due to close proximity and gravity, the planet was ripped apart.
