SPECULOOS-3




Stats

  • Planet(s): SPECULOOS-3b

  • Star Type: M Type Red Dwarf

  • Diameter: 186,000km

  • Mass: 5 × 10²⁶ metric tons

  • Surface Temperature: 2,873K

  • Absolute Magnitude: 16.8

  • Distance from Earth: 55 light-years

  • Discovery Date: 5/15/2023

  • Discovery Method: Transit Photometry

Description

SPECULOOS-3 is a ultra-cool Red Dawrf type star. It is a cool star, more than twice as cold as our Sun. It is also small, only around a tenth of the Sun's mass. SPECULOOS-3 will likely be a long-living star (has already lived 6.6 billion years), since it is an ultra-cool star, and won't burn as much hydrogen fuel. SPECULOOS-3 is the second-smallest star to have a exoplanet orbiting it (with the first being TRAPPIST-1).
