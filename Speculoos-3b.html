Description

SPECULOOS-3b is terrestrial-type planet, with a mass of 98% of Earth's. While it hasn't been confirmed, there is evidence that suggests that SPECULOOS-3b is tidally locked (similarly to TRAPPIST-1b). One fun fact is that the SPECULOOS-3 system is only the second planetary system revolving around an ultracool Red Dwarf. Due to close proximity to its star, immense radiation floods SPECULOOS-3b, causing a devoid atmosphere.
