Description

TRAPPIST-1 is a cool Red Dwarf type star. It is a very, very, dim star, its luminoisty being only around 1/200,000th of the Sun. Not only is it dim, it's also small. It's diameter is only 12% that of the Sun. By star standards, TRAPPIST-1 is cold, 2000 Kelvin less than the Sun. TRAPPIST-1 is decently close star, only about 40 light-years away.
